About CNM 2026

The Convolvulaceae Network Meeting 2026 (CNM 2026) brings together researchers in systematics, evolution, ecology, genomics, and conservation of Convolvulaceae. The meeting will be held at the Instituto de Biología, UNAM, in Mexico City from October 26 to 30, 2026.

Use the links above to register, view the program, get travel directions, and download flyers. For questions, contact convomeetingmx@gmail.com.
